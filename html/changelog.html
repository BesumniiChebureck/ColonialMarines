alardun updated:

  • Added the Gardener Strain for the drone. Ability details down below. Thanks Triiodine for all of the sprites. It benefits from slightly (5%) increased health regen.
  • Ability 1: Plant Fruit. (Macro 3). Plant an instance of the currently selected fruit (up to 3 fruits total). Consumes 50 health and 100 plasma. These fruits take some time to mature, and can be consumed by Xenomorph to gain an effect depending on the fruit's type. (See below)
  • Added Greater Resin Fruit. This fruit heals 75 health instantly, and rapidly heal another 100 over a few ticks. It takes 30s to mature.
  • Added Unstable Resin Fruit. This fruit heals 75 health over a longer # of ticks, and also gives an overshield for 30% of max health, up to 200. This shield begins to decay after 90 seconds, at a rate of 10 per second. Does not stack with itself. It takes 45s to mature.
  • Added Spore Resin Fruit. This fruit passively gives off Level 1 Recovery pheromones in a range of one tile, and when consumed, makes each slashes increase your cooldown reduction by 5%, up to 25%. This buff lasts for 60 seconds. This mean any future cooldown will be reduced by that percentage. It takes 15s to mature.
  • Ability 2: Change Fruit. (Macro 4) Cycle between Greater/Unstable/Spore.
  • Ability 3: Resin Surge. (Macro 2) Depending on the tile targeted, has a different effect. 10 seconds cooldown. 75 Plasma cost. See below:
  • Resin surge on a Resin Door or Wall gives it a temporary 6000 shield bonus for 3 seconds. Prevents Hivelord from thickening the structure for the duration.
  • Resin surge on a fruit reduce the time to maturation by 5s.
  • Resin surge on a weeded tile creates a 100 health wall for 5s. This wall cannot be thickened.
  • Resin surge on a unweeded tile creates sticky resins (3x3) at a location for 20s. These are weaker than regular sticky resin.
  • Added Lesser Resin Fruit. This fruit heals 75 health instantly when consumed.
  • Healer Drone also gain Plant Fruit, but may only plant Lesser Resin Fruit.
  • Taking damage while shielded as a Xeno will do a different sound effect. Thanks Triiodine for the sounds effect
  • Losing your xeno shield to damages will do a shattering sound.
  • Altered Vanguard shielding logic a little. There should be no difference in actual gameplay, so ping me if you notice something amiss.
  • Altered Shielding logic in general.

Vanagandr updated:

  • Hypospray reworked, autoinjectors tweaked.
  • Bug with attempting to remove unlocked hyposprays from inventory pulling the vial out fixed. Bugs with hyposprays filling from any reagent container fixed.
  • Hyposprays and autoinjector sfx are a bit more audible now. Emergency autoinjectors now have a very distinct sfx.

  • Examining a hypospray now displays its vial and remaining reagent volume, if any, and its current dose setting.
  • The hypo now handles much like a pistol; hold it and click it with an empty hand to remove the chemical vial; hold an unloaded hypo and click it with a vial to load it.
  • If you have medical skills, you can click a vial with an unloaded hypospray, or click-drag a vial to an unloaded hypo, to perform a 'tactical reload'. This will transfer the old vial into your other hand, or drop the old vial on the ground if your hands are full.
  • The hypo can still be filled without removing the vial; it fills like any other beaker or flask, including pill dissolving, reagent tanks, and transfers from other vials. Of course, you can't transfer chemicals into a hypospray that doesn't have a vial.
  • The top-up port has a one-way valve; if you want to remove reagents from a hypospray, either syringe it or remove the vial.
  • For the most part, this is all *additional* functionality - you can still use it in the same way you did before without any problems.
  • I've tested this repeatedly and it all works as expected, but if you notice hypos or autoinjectors behaving strangely, @ me.
